# Human Spiritual Universe · 人类精神宇宙

The Atlas of Belief, Consciousness, Myth & Meaning — a world-class bilingual (English / 中文)
exploration of humanity's search for meaning: belief systems, religions, philosophies, mythologies,
mysticism, and consciousness, mapped as one interconnected cosmos. Educational and comparative —
**not** a religious or conversion platform.

25 sections (from "The Map of Belief" through "The Future of Spirituality"), an interactive
**Cosmic Knowledge Graph** linking traditions, concepts, figures, symbols, and texts, and an
**AI Cosmic Guide** that sets the world's traditions side by side on the biggest questions.

## Stack

Next.js 14 (App Router, client-rendered SPA to avoid hydration issues), TypeScript, custom cosmic
design system (dark mode, glassmorphism, sacred geometry, constellation canvases). Bilingual via a
language context with an instant global toggle. Content is typed `Section` data; visualizations
(constellation maps, timelines, comparison tables, knowledge graph) are reusable canvas components.
